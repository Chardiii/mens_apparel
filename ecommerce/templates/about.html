Who We Are

Mode S7vn is a local e-commerce platform built to connect buyers, sellers, and riders in one seamless experience. We believe shopping should be simple — browse, order, and pay only when your item arrives at your door.

🛍️
Buyers

Browse products, add to cart, and checkout. Pay cash when your rider arrives.

🏪
Sellers

List your products, verify incoming orders, and hand them off to our riders.

🏍️
Riders

Pick up verified orders from sellers and deliver them to buyers. Collect cash on delivery.

Why Cash on Delivery?

We keep it simple and trustworthy. No payment APIs, no card details, no online transactions. You only pay when your order is physically in your hands — verified by the seller, delivered by our rider.
